# Employee Policy Compliance Requirements

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Last Reviewed:** February 21, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** Chief Human Resources Officer  
**Applicable Regulations:** Labor Laws, OSHA, Company Handbook

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the compliance requirements for employee conduct, attendance, training, and workplace standards. All employees MUST adhere to these requirements to maintain a compliant and productive work environment.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- All full-time and part-time employees
- All contractors and temporary workers
- All departments and business units
- All office locations and remote workers

## 3. Attendance Compliance

### 3.1 Attendance Rate Requirements

**MANDATORY COMPLIANCE THRESHOLDS:**

| Employee Type | Minimum Attendance Rate | Warning Threshold | Critical Threshold |
| ------------- | ----------------------- | ----------------- | ------------------ |
| Full-time     | 95%                     | 90%               | 85%                |
| Part-time     | 90%                     | 85%               | 80%                |
| Probationary  | 98%                     | 95%               | 90%                |

- Attendance rate below **85%** for full-time employees is a **CRITICAL** violation
- Three consecutive months below 90% attendance triggers Performance Improvement Plan
- Attendance data MUST be reviewed monthly by department managers

### 3.2 Time Recording Requirements

All employees MUST:

- Record arrival time within **15 minutes** of scheduled start
- Record departure time within **15 minutes** of scheduled end
- Account for all breaks exceeding **30 minutes**
- Report any discrepancies within **24 hours**

### 3.3 Absence Reporting

- Planned absence: Request submitted **at least 2 weeks** in advance
- Unplanned absence: Notification within **1 hour** of scheduled start
- Medical absence > 3 days: Medical certificate required
- Unapproved absence: Automatic **MEDIUM** violation flag

## 4. Leave Policy Compliance

### 4.1 Annual Leave Entitlements

| Tenure     | Annual Leave Days | Carry-over Limit |
| ---------- | ----------------- | ---------------- |
| 0-2 years  | 20 days           | 5 days           |
| 2-5 years  | 23 days           | 7 days           |
| 5-10 years | 25 days           | 10 days          |
| 10+ years  | 28 days           | 12 days          |

### 4.2 Leave Policy Violations

**VIOLATION CONDITIONS:**

1. **Leave Overage**
   - Using more than allocated leave days is a **HIGH** violation
   - Unauthorized leave taken results in pay deduction
   - Exceeding carry-over limit forfeits excess days

2. **Leave Abuse**
   - Pattern of Monday/Friday absences
   - Absence around holidays without approval
   - Three or more last-minute cancellations in 90 days

### 4.3 Special Leave Compliance

- Sick leave: Maximum **10 days** without medical certificate
- Bereavement: Maximum **5 days** with documentation
- Jury duty: Full time off with court documentation
- Parental leave: As per statutory requirements

## 5. Mandatory Training Compliance

### 5.1 Required Training Programs

**ALL EMPLOYEES MUST COMPLETE:**

| Training           | Frequency | Deadline                           | Violation Level |
| ------------------ | --------- | ---------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Security Awareness | Annual    | Within 30 days of due date         | HIGH            |
| Code of Conduct    | Annual    | Within 30 days of hire anniversary | MEDIUM          |
| Anti-Harassment    | Annual    | Within 60 days of due date         | HIGH            |
| Data Privacy       | Annual    | Within 30 days of due date         | CRITICAL        |
| Safety Training    | Biennial  | Within 45 days of due date         | MEDIUM          |

### 5.2 Role-Specific Training

| Role    | Required Training          | Completion Window    |
| ------- | -------------------------- | -------------------- |
| Manager | Leadership & HR Compliance | 60 days of promotion |
| Finance | SOX Compliance             | 30 days of hire      |
| IT      | Security Certification     | 90 days of hire      |
| Sales   | Product & Compliance       | 45 days of hire      |

### 5.3 Training Compliance Violations

- Incomplete training after deadline: **MEDIUM** violation
- Training not started 14 days before deadline: Warning issued
- Three incomplete trainings: Escalation to HR Director
- Training completion required for annual performance review eligibility

## 6. Security and Access Compliance

### 6.1 Security Clearance Requirements

- Security clearance MUST be valid and current
- Expired clearance (> 30 days) is a **HIGH** violation
- Clearance renewal MUST be initiated 60 days before expiration
- Access revoked immediately upon clearance expiration

### 6.2 NDA and Confidentiality

**MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS:**

- NDA MUST be signed within **7 days** of hire
- Annual confidentiality acknowledgment required
- Missing NDA is a **CRITICAL** compliance violation
- NDA violations result in immediate termination

### 6.3 Badge and Access Controls

- Badge MUST be visible at all times on premises
- Lost badge reported within **4 hours**
- Tailgating or badge sharing: **HIGH** violation
- Badge not returned on termination: Final paycheck hold

## 7. Performance Review Compliance

### 7.1 Review Schedule

| Review Type       | Frequency    | Maximum Days Overdue |
| ----------------- | ------------ | -------------------- |
| Annual Review     | Yearly       | 30 days              |
| Probation Review  | 90 days      | 14 days              |
| Mid-year Check-in | 6 months     | 21 days              |
| PIP Review        | As scheduled | 7 days               |

### 7.2 Review Compliance Requirements

- Manager MUST complete reviews by deadline
- Overdue review > 30 days: Manager receives **MEDIUM** violation
- Employee MUST acknowledge review within **7 days**
- Missing reviews block merit increases

## 8. Compliance Scoring

### 8.1 Compliance Score Calculation

Each employee has a compliance score (0-100):

| Component                 | Weight |
| ------------------------- | ------ |
| Attendance Rate           | 30%    |
| Training Completion       | 25%    |
| Leave Policy Adherence    | 15%    |
| Security Compliance       | 20%    |
| Performance Documentation | 10%    |

### 8.2 Score Thresholds

| Score Range | Status    | Action Required                    |
| ----------- | --------- | ---------------------------------- |
| 90-100      | Excellent | Eligible for compliance bonus      |
| 80-89       | Good      | Standard standing                  |
| 70-79       | Fair      | Manager discussion required        |
| 60-69       | Warning   | HR notification, PIP consideration |
| < 60        | Critical  | Immediate HR intervention          |

## 9. Violation Handling

### 9.1 Violation Severity

| Severity | Examples                                                    | Consequences         |
| -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------- |
| CRITICAL | Missing NDA, data breach, harassment                        | Immediate suspension |
| HIGH     | Security violation, leave abuse, training > 60 days overdue | Written warning      |
| MEDIUM   | Attendance < 85%, minor policy breach                       | Verbal warning       |
| LOW      | Administrative oversights                                   | Documentation only   |

### 9.2 Appeals Process

- Employees may appeal violations within **14 days**
- Appeals reviewed by HR and direct manager's supervisor
- Decision communicated within **21 days**
- Final appeal to HR Director within **7 days** of initial decision

## 10. Reporting and Monitoring

### 10.1 Automated Monitoring

The compliance system MUST:

- Track all attendance records in real-time
- Monitor training completion status
- Calculate compliance scores weekly
- Generate violation alerts automatically

### 10.2 Manager Responsibilities

- Review team compliance dashboard weekly
- Address violations within SLA timeframes
- Conduct monthly compliance check-ins
- Escalate patterns of non-compliance
